About Kala Tech

At Kala Tech, we believe that small businesses are the backbone of our economy, and they deserve the same access to cutting-edge technology as larger corporations. Our mission is to empower small businesses by providing the tools, guidance, and support they need to thrive in today’s rapidly evolving digital landscape.

Meet the Founder

Arron McCrory, the founder of Kala Tech, has over a decade of experience working with some of the largest companies in the corporate world. Throughout his career, Arron has seen first-hand how powerful technology can be in transforming businesses, streamlining operations, and driving success.

Arron’s passion for helping small businesses succeed led him to start Kala Tech. He recognized the unique challenges small businesses face when trying to keep up with technological advancements, and he set out to create a company dedicated to bridging that gap. Arron believes that every business, no matter its size, deserves the opportunity to harness technology to grow, compete, and thrive.
